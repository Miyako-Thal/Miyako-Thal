The Process

Four stops. One clear line.

A mandate runs like a rail line a fixed route, no detours. Each stop has a defined deliverable before the next one begins.

M-01

Sourcing

Tenanted 1R/1K units, 15–30 m², within 10 minutes' walk of a station, from vetted local brokers.

M-02

Due diligence

Building management records, repair-fund health, tenancy status and yield verification against a fixed checklist.

M-03

Closing

Contract coordination, translation and remote completion support. The title is registered in your name.

M-04

Oversight

Local property management in place, monthly reporting in English, one point of contact: us.

The Mandate Model

No fund. No pooling. No blind risk.

This is not an investment vehicle. It is a buy-side service: you acquire a specific, named asset directly, and we are paid for delivering the transaction not for holding your capital.

Direct ownership

The unit is registered to you or your own holding structure. No commingled capital, no intermediary vehicle between you and the asset.

Success-based economics

Our acquisition fee is payable only at completion. If the transaction does not close, the mandate costs you nothing beyond the initial search retainer which is credited against the fee at closing.

Transparent, single-asset scope

Every mandate covers one named unit with its purchase price, current rent and net yield stated up front. You decide on a real asset, not a projection.
